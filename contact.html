Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up as a volunteer?

Click the "Sign Up" button and select "Volunteer for events" to create your volunteer account. You'll then be able to browse and register for events.

How can I create an event?

Sign up as an organizer and access your dashboard to create and manage events. You can set event details, manage volunteers, and track registrations.

Is there a cost to use VolunteerHub?

VolunteerHub is completely free for both volunteers and organizers. Our mission is to make volunteering accessible to everyone.

How do I cancel my event registration?

Go to your volunteer dashboard and find the event in "My Events". Click "Unregister" to cancel your participation.
